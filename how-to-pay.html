How to Pay

A step-by-step guide to making secure and seamless payments on Pay Jigawa.

1. Access the Pay Jigawa Home Page

Go to the Pay Jigawa website and click on "Generate Invoice".

2. Identify Yourself

Choose one of the following to verify your identity:

  • ✅ TIN (Taxpayer Identification Number)
  • ✅ Tax Registration ID
  • ✅ Email
  • ✅ Phone Number

3. First-Time Users

If this is your first time using Pay Jigawa, you’ll need to manually enter your details.

4. Enter Your Details

Fill in your personal information and proceed.

5. Add Billing Information

Select the applicable billing details. You can add multiple items if needed, then proceed to generate your invoice.

6. Generate and Preview Invoice

Once your invoice is created, click the "Preview" button to review your details before payment.

7. Make Payment

On the invoice preview page, click "Make Payment" at the bottom.

8. Choose a Payment Method

Select your preferred payment channel and complete your transaction.
